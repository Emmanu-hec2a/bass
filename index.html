Curriculum

Academic Structure

The 8-4-4 system representing 8 years of primary school, 4 years of secondary school and 4 years of university was the old traditional structure. However, the school is transitioning to a 2-6-3-3-3 system of the Competency Based Curriculum(CBC) representing 2 years of pre-primary, 6 years of primary, 3 years of junior secondary, 3 years of senior secondary and 3 years of university education.
